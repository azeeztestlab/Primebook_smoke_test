## 1. Executive Summary
PrimBooks is an all-in-one, real-time, cloud-based financial and business management platform. It is designed to replace fragmented systems (spreadsheets, notebooks, disconnected tools) with a unified operating system for SMEs.

### Core Value Proposition:
- **Unified Platform:** Connects finance, operations, compliance, and HR.
- **Real-Time Visibility:** Cloud-based access to financial health from anywhere.
- **User-Centric Design:** Simple for non-accountants, structured enough for auditors.

---

## 2. Strategic Objectives

### Vision
To build the most intelligent and accessible financial operating system for SMEs worldwide.

### Mission
To empower businesses with financial clarity, operational efficiency, and scalable control so they can make data-driven decisions with confidence.

---

## 3. Core Problems Solved
1. **Fragmentation:** Centralizes records currently scattered across spreadsheets and notebooks.
2. **Reconciliation:** Provides a stable, reliable workflow for bank reconciliation (Core Selling Point).
3. **Accountability:** Built-in audit trails to track "who did what."
4. **Asset Tracking:** Structured management of depreciation and asset transactions.
5. **Operational Gap:** Connects CRM, Inventory, and Production directly to the financial engine.

---

## 4. MVP Module Breakdown

### 4.1 Dashboard & Record
- **Dashboard:** Real-time KPI cards, business insight widgets, and cash flow charts. Supports Dark/Light/System modes.
- **Record:** The foundational entry point for all financial and operational transactions.

### 4.2 CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
- **Orders:** Create, pay, cancel, deliver, delete. (View/Edit/Export pending).
- **Invoices:** UI approved; frontend implementation in progress.
- **Customers:** Activation, role management, and email integration.
- **Quotations:** Creation and approval workflow.

### 4.3 Production & Inventory
- **Production:** Full assembly flow with Bill of Materials (BOM) and stock usage tracking.
- **Inventory:** Tracking stock movement and adjustments (Implementation pending).

### 4.4 Finance & Bank Reconciliation
- **Accounting Engine:** Chart of Accounts, Journals, Banking, and Taxation modules.
- **Reconciliation:** Statement upload, transaction matching, and automated posting of outstanding items.

### 4.5 HR & Payroll
- **Workforce Mgmt:** Employee records, attendance, and leave management.
- **Payroll:** Salary processing and loan management.

### 4.6 Assets & Reports
- **Assets:** Acquisition, disposal, and depreciation tracking.
- **Reports:** Automated Sales, Expense, Vendor, and Customer reports (Frontend pending).

---

## 5. Technical Requirements
- **Architecture:** Progressive Web App (PWA) for mobile-like experience without a native app.
- **Security:** Role-based permissions (Admin, Accountant, HR, Auditor) and full audit logging.
- **Compliance:** Built-in support for Nigerian tax laws (VAT, WHT).

---

## 6. Post-Launch Roadmap

### Phase 1: Budgeting Module
- Zero-base/Flexible/Fixed budgeting.
- Variance tracking (Budget vs. Actual) with over-budget alerts.

### Phase 2: PrimAI (AI Financial Assistant)
- Automated break-even and ratio analysis.
- Cash flow forecasting and revenue drop detection.

### Phase 3: Workforce Automation
- Biometric integration and employee self-service.
- Automatic loan approval logic.

### Phase 4: Scaling & Multi-Currency
- FX rate tracking and financial statement translation.
- Advanced customer/vendor statement export.
